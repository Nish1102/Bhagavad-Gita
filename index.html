Bhagavad Gita

The Bhagavad Gita teaches us the mastery of our own minds, the importance of pursuing our dharma truthfully and unapologetically, and how to embrace life for what it is.

"Yada yada hi dharmasya glaanirbhavati bhaarat
Abhyutthaanam adharmasya tadaatmaanam srijaamyaham
Paritranaay saadhunaam vinaashaay cha dushkritaam
Dharm sansthaapanaarthaay sambhavaami yuge yuge"

Translation: "Whenever there is a decline in righteousness and an increase in unrighteousness, O Bharata (Arjuna), at that time I manifest myself on earth.
To protect the righteous, to annihilate the wicked, and to reestablish the principles of dharma (righteousness), I appear millennium after millennium."
